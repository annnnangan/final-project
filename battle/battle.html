Rules

Beat your opponent by getting as much live cells as you could

  1. There is only 1 round in a games. To start a new around, please click "Reset Game".
  2. To start, each player should click "Start Game" button and will be given 10 seconds to drag on the board.
  3. After 10 seconds, you will be entered 10 seconds generating time, which you are not allowed to drew and the cell will generate at this time.
  4. Score will be counted afterward.
  5. Player with more live cells will have higher score and win the games.
